Seedream 5 Pro prompt FAQ

What makes a good Seedream 5 Pro prompt?

A good Seedream 5 Pro prompt names the subject, scene, composition, style, constraints, and quality requirements in clear language.

Can I copy these Seedream 5 Pro prompts?

Yes. Use them as examples, keep attribution when a source is provided, and adapt the subject, brand, scene, or text to your project.

How should I adapt a prompt for Seedream 5 Pro?

Keep the core structure, then replace the subject, scene, aspect ratio, style, and text rules with your own requirements.
