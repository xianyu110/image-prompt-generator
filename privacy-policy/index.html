Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Information We Process

Image Prompt Generator does not require user accounts. When you use the prompt generator, the subject text, selected model, aspect ratio, and interface language may be sent to the site API to return a prompt. Do not submit private, confidential, or sensitive information in prompt requests.

Standard server logs may include request time, URL, browser information, IP-derived network information, and error details needed to operate and protect the site.

Analytics and Advertising

The site uses Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic and page usage. The site also uses Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads, measure ad performance, and limit ad frequency.

You can learn how Google uses information from sites and apps that use its services at Google's partner sites policy. You can manage ad personalization in Google Ads Settings.

Cookies and Local Storage

The site may use cookies from Google services. It also uses browser local storage to remember interface choices such as language, theme, and background color.

Third-Party Links

Prompt cards may link to external sources, including public repositories or creator posts. Those third-party sites have their own privacy practices.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact options on the Contact page.
