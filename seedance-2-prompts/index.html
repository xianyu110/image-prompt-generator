Seedance 2.0 prompt FAQ

What makes a good Seedance 2.0 prompt?

A good Seedance 2.0 prompt names the subject, scene, composition, style, constraints, and quality requirements in clear language.

Can I copy these Seedance 2.0 prompts?

Yes. Use them as examples, keep attribution when a source is provided, and adapt the subject, brand, scene, or text to your project.

How should I adapt a prompt for Seedance 2.0?

Keep the core structure, then replace the subject, scene, aspect ratio, style, and text rules with your own requirements.
