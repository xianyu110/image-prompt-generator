GPT Image 2 prompt FAQ

What makes a good GPT Image 2 prompt?

A good GPT Image 2 prompt names the subject, scene, composition, style, constraints, and quality requirements in clear language.

Can I copy these GPT Image 2 prompts?

Yes. Use them as examples, keep attribution when a source is provided, and adapt the subject, brand, scene, or text to your project.

How should I adapt a prompt for GPT Image 2?

Keep the core structure, then replace the subject, scene, aspect ratio, style, and text rules with your own requirements.
